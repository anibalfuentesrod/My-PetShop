Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how My Petshop collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Your name
  • Contact information (e.g., email address, phone number)
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment details (processed securely through our payment providers)
  • Details of your orders

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order confirmation and updates
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send promotional offers and newsletters (if you opt in)

3. Protecting Your Information

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We implement various security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or distribute your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with third-party providers involved in processing your orders (e.g., payment gateways or shipping services).

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. A cookie is a small file placed on your computer to help us analyze web traffic and remember your preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, update or correct your information, and request deletion of your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of those changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@mysite.com.
