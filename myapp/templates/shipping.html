Shipping Policy

At My Petshop, we aim to deliver your furry friend's needs quickly and safely to your doorstep. Please read through our shipping policy below for more information.

Processing Time

  • All orders are processed within 1-3 business days.
  • Orders are not shipped on weekends or holidays.
  • If there is a delay, we will notify you promptly via email.

Shipping Rates and Delivery Time

Shipping Method Cost Estimated Delivery Time
Standard Shipping $5.00 3-5 Business Days
Express Shipping $15.00 1-2 Business Days
Free Shipping (Orders Over $50) $0.00 3-5 Business Days

Shipping Restrictions

  • We currently ship only within the United States.
  • We do not ship to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor the delivery status of your package.

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding shipping, please contact us at support@mypetshop.com.
