Eligibility for Refund

  • Items must be unused and in the same condition as received.

How to Request a Refund

If you meet the above criteria, please contact us within 30 days of your purchase to initiate the refund process.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund.

  • If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days.
  • The amount will be automatically applied to your original method of payment.

Exceptions

  • Perishable goods such as food and treats cannot be refunded.
  • Gift cards are non-refundable.
