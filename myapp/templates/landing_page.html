About My PetShop

My partner and I are passionate animal lovers. With my father running a pet shop, we were inspired to build a beautiful, engaging web application that reflects our love for pets.

Our challenge was to design something that is not only functional but also eye-catching and fun. We wanted to capture the joy of being a pet owner in every detail of this project.

This project also serves as a portfolio project for Holberton School Puerto Rico.
